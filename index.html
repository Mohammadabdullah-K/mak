  • What are the benefits of a HydraFacial?

    The benefits of a HydraFacial include:

    • Improved skin texture: A HydraFacial can help to improve the texture of the skin by removing dead skin cells and debris.
    • Reduced pore size: A HydraFacial can help to reduce the appearance of pore size by extracting blackheads and whiteheads.
    • Increased hydration: A HydraFacial can help to hydrate the skin by delivering serums and moisturizers.
    • Improved radiance: A HydraFacial can help to improve the radiance of the skin by removing dead skin cells and debris.
    • Reduced signs of aging: A HydraFacial can help to reduce the signs of aging, such as fine lines and wrinkles, by exfoliating the skin and delivering serums and moisturizers.

  • Who is a good candidate for a HydraFacial?

    A HydraFacial is a good option for people with all skin types, including sensitive skin. However, it is important to talk to a dermatologist or esthetician before getting a HydraFacial if you have any underlying skin conditions.

  • How long does a HydraFacial take?

    A HydraFacial typically takes about 2 hours.

  • Are the results of HydraFacial immediate?

    The results of a HydraFacial are typically visible immediately. However, the full results may take a few weeks to appear.

  • Is there any downtime after a Hydrafacial?

    There is no downtime after a Hydrafacial. You can resume your normal activities immediately after the treatment.

  • Is a Hydrafacial painful?

    A Hydrafacial is generally not painful.
