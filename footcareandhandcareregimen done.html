Footcare & Handcare Regimen

  1. Home
  2. Footcare & Handcare Regimen

 The foot/hand care regimen at Dermlax includes the following therapies:

  • Diamond crystal dermabrasion
  • High frequency therapy
  • Chemical peel
  • Pedicure/manicure

Beneficial for:

  • Dead skin
  • Dark patches caused by friction/trauma/waxing
  • Uneven skin tone
  • Skin softening
  • Rough, ashy, dry skin
  • Dark toes/knuckles
  • Sun tan

Diamond Crystal Dermabrasion

This treatment is used to gently exfoliate the outermost layer of the skin. The wand is diamond-tipped and, as the wand is moved across the skin, the diamond chips abrade the surface, removing dead skin cells. This process stimulates blood flow to the skin and encourages the production of collagen and elastin, two proteins that are essential for maintaining the skin's elasticity and firmness.

High Frequency Therapy

The electrical current produced by the high-frequency device has antimicrobial properties, which can help to kill bacteria and fungi on the skin of the feet. This makes it an effective treatment for foot conditions such as athlete's foot and fungal infections. It also helps to improve blood circulation in the feet, hence improving skin health, appearance, and quality.

Chemical Peel

A gentle acid is applied to remove the outermost layer of dead and damaged skin cells from the surface of the skin. This process promotes the growth of new, healthy skin cells, resulting in smoother, more even-toned skin. The chemical exfoliating agents work by breaking down the bonds between dead skin cells, allowing them to be easily sloughed off to reveal fresh skin.

Pedicure/Manicure

  • Hydroquinone bleach
  • Warm water soak
  • Scrub & cleanse
  • Nails filing & cleaning
  • Exfoliate
  • Massage

Sessions:

  • 5-8 sessions at 4 weeks gap.
  • Then maintenance sessions can be taken 2-4 monthly depending on your lifestyle and care.
  • Regular homecare as suggested by the doctor is necessary for the results to show.

Painless:

  • No downtime
  • No side effects
